About me

I am currently pursuing my M.Sc. degree in control science and engineering with the School of Automation at Guangdong University of Technology and supervised by Prof. Yong Xu. My previous research focuses on finite-, fixed-, and predefined-time consensus for multiagent systems via discontinuous control, with potential applications in formation control as well as attitude synchronization. I serve as a reviewer for Automatica and Neurocomputing.

I am now seeking a Ph.D. position for Fall 2023!
