About me

I am currently pursuing my M.Sc. degree in control science and engineering with the School of Automation, Guangdong University of Technology, advised by Prof. Yong Xu. I serve as reviewer for Automatica and Neurocomputing. My previous research focuses on finite-, fixed-, and predefined-time consensus for multi-agent systems via discontinuous control, with potential applications in formation control as well as attitude synchronization. I am now seeking a Ph.D. position in multiagent systems theory for Fall 2023.

  • Yong Xu, Zhaozhan Yao, Renquan Lu, and Bijoy K. Ghosh, A novel fixed-time protocol for first-order consensus tracking with disturbance rejection, IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, to appear.
